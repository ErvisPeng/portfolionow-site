Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

PortfolioNow values your privacy.

We understand the sensitivity of investment information and have adopted a "local-first" design philosophy to ensure your data remains secure.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

PortfolioNow does not collect, store, or transmit the following information:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Portfolio data (holdings, transaction records, gains/losses, etc.)
  • Device identification information
  • Usage behavior data

Data Storage

All data is stored only on your device and your personal iCloud account.

All investment data you enter, including:

  • Holdings information
  • Transaction records
  • Portfolio history snapshots

is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

iCloud Sync

PortfolioNow uses Apple's CloudKit service to provide iCloud sync functionality:

  • Automatic Backup - Your data is automatically synced to your personal iCloud account
  • Multi-Device Sync - Seamlessly sync across iPhone, iPad, Mac, and other devices
  • End-to-End Encryption - Data transmission and storage are protected by Apple's security mechanisms

iCloud sync data is stored only in your personal Apple account, and we cannot access this data. To disable sync, go to iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud and turn off iCloud access for this app.

Network Connection

PortfolioNow only connects to the network in the following situations:

  • Fetching real-time stock prices - Through third-party financial data APIs to obtain stock quotes. This process does not transmit your personal data or portfolio information.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services to obtain stock price information:

  • Yahoo Finance API - Used to fetch real-time stock prices

We do not send any information that could identify you or reveal your portfolio contents to these services.

Data Security

To protect your data security, PortfolioNow provides:

  • Face ID / Touch ID Lock - Prevents unauthorized access
  • Amount Hiding Feature - Protects sensitive numbers in public places
  • Local Storage - Data never leaves your device

Data Export and Backup

You can export all your data in JSON format at any time through the export feature in the app to a location of your choice. The exported file is completely under your control.

Children's Privacy

PortfolioNow is not designed for children under 13 years of age, and we do not intentionally collect any information from children.

Policy Updates

If there are any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page. We recommend that you check this page regularly for the latest information.
