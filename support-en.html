Getting Started

1. Add a Holding

Tap the "+" button in the top right corner of the main screen and enter a stock symbol (e.g., AAPL, TSLA) to add a holding. The app will automatically fetch real-time prices.

2. Record Transactions

On the holding detail page, tap "Add Transaction," select the transaction type (buy, sell, dividend, etc.), and enter the date, quantity, and price to complete the record.

3. Import Broker Data

Go to "Settings" ‚Üí "Data Management," select your broker (Firstrade or Charles Schwab), and follow the instructions to import your transaction history.

4. Export Backup

In "Settings" ‚Üí "Data Management," tap "Export Data" to backup all your data in JSON format.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data uploaded to the cloud?
A: No. PortfolioNow uses a "local-first" design, meaning all data is stored on your device and is never uploaded to any server.
Q: How often are stock prices updated?
A: Stock prices are fetched from Yahoo Finance API and update automatically when you open the app or pull down to refresh. Please note that prices may be delayed by 15-20 minutes.
Q: How do I hide amounts?
A: Tap the eye icon in the top right corner of the main screen to toggle the visibility of amounts. This is especially useful in public places.
Q: Which brokers are supported for data import?
A: Currently, we support Firstrade (CSV format) and Charles Schwab (JSON format). We will continue to add support for more brokers.
Q: How do I enable Face ID / Touch ID?
A: Go to "Settings" ‚Üí "Security" and turn on the Face ID or Touch ID toggle. Authentication will be required each time you open the app.
Q: How do I transfer data when changing phones?
A: If you have iCloud sync enabled, your data will automatically sync to your new device. You can also use the "Export Data" feature to backup your data as a JSON file, then use the "Import Data" feature on your new phone to restore.
Q: Does the app support options?
A: Yes, PortfolioNow fully supports tracking and recording US stock options (Call/Put), including expiration date tracking, P&L calculation, and more.
Q: How do I switch cost calculation methods?
A: Go to "Settings" ‚Üí "Cost Calculation Method" and choose between FIFO (First In First Out) or Average Cost. The system will automatically recalculate all realized P&L after switching.
Q: How does iCloud sync work?
A: When you're signed into your Apple ID with iCloud enabled, your data automatically syncs to your iCloud account and stays in sync across all your devices. Data is stored only in your personal iCloud account, and we cannot access it.
Q: How do I use widgets?
A: Long press on an empty area of your Home Screen to enter edit mode, tap the "+" button in the top left corner, and search for "PortfolioNow" to add a widget. Various sizes are supported for Lock Screen and Home Screen.
Q: What features does the Apple Watch app have?
A: The Apple Watch app lets you quickly view your portfolio overview, holdings list, and real-time P&L. It also supports Watch Complications, so you can check market updates with a glance at your wrist.

Troubleshooting

Stock Prices Not Updating

  • Make sure your device is connected to the internet
  • Try pulling down to refresh or restarting the app
  • Some stock symbols may require an exchange suffix (e.g., .HK, .TW)

Import Failed

  • Make sure the file format is correct (CSV for Firstrade, JSON for Charles Schwab)
  • Ensure the file has not been modified or corrupted
  • Try re-downloading the transaction history from your broker's website

Face ID Not Working

  • Make sure your device supports Face ID or Touch ID
  • Go to iOS Settings to confirm Face ID is properly configured
  • Try turning the feature off and on again in the app settings
